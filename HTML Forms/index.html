Ordered List: Steps to Prepare a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil Water: Heat water in a kettle until it reaches the boiling point.
  2. Add Tea Leaves or Tea Bag: Place tea leaves in a strainer or a tea bag in your cup.
  3. Pour Hot Water: Pour the boiled water into the cup and let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Sweeten or Add Milk (Optional): Add sugar, honey, or milk if desired, and stir well.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Sip and savor your freshly made tea.
